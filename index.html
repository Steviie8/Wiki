About me

Hi, I'm Steven

My name is Steven Xu, and I am a high school student about to graduate. I have spent the past few years playing music at elderly homes, which has allowed me to become the president of a group of students at WeCareActPearland. Through my leadership, we have raised money for alzheimers and improved our community through music. To lead more into my interests, I have spent the past year in robotics (team Pearadox 5414), allowing me to better understand the real-world skills in engineering: iterating, design, documentation, and CAD skills. Along with working in robotics, I have started to work on my own projects with microcontrollers; I have created screens that use API’s to display data on a 32x64-bit screen and RC cars that I used CAD to make an enclosure. Along with all this hardware knowledge I have gained, I have also worked in a lot of software, learning Python, Java, C++, and GDScript.

More about my hobbies, I very much enjoy working with microcontrollers, allowing me to create thousands of trinkets and technology. I also enjoy making games as it allows me to use my mind to create and communicate with others to be able to release fun and engaging products that I am proud of. Finally, the last thing that I very much enjoy is photography, allowing me to snap memories and create masterpieces that I can use to express myself.
